NATIONAL GELLERY INSIDER IS NATIONAL GALLERY SINGAPORE'S MEMBERSHIP PROGRAMME

With two permanent galleries, a line-up of special exhibitions and international collaborations, nine dining outlets, and countless programmes and activities, there's plenty to see and do at the Gallery.

Enjoy special privileges as a member of the National Gallery Singapore family. Gallery Insiders enjoy benefits including unlimited free entry to all exhibitions, priority booking and discounts on dining, shopping, and ticketed events at the Gallery.

Simply choose the membership type which best suits your needs. Or if you know someone who loves art, surprise them with a gift membership so that they can enjoy art all year round.
